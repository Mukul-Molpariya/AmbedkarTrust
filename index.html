National Br. Ambedkar Education , Sports , And Employment Charitable Trust

Freedom Of Minde Is Real Freedom

Dr. B.R. Ambedkar, a visionary leader and champion of social justice in India, left an indelible mark on the nation's history by tirelessly advocating for education, employment opportunities, and the promotion of sports to uplift marginalized communities, particularly the Dalits.

OUR MISSION

"With a focus on skill development , real-world job experiebnce , and trauma-informed care , Hope works Propels young adults into long term living wage carrers that put them on the path for healing and financial stability.

OUR VISSION

Our vision is to follow in Dr. B.R. Ambedkar's footsteps, promoting education, employment, and sports as tools for social justice and empowerment in India. We aim to create an inclusive society where everyone has equal access to these opportunities, regardless of their background.
